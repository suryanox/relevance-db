Data Retention Policy — Acme Corp
Last updated: March 2024

The data retention policy was formally reviewed and updated following the Q1 2024 audit.
Alice Chen, Head of Compliance, approved the new guidelines on March 15 2024.
The audit was conducted by the Risk and Compliance team led by Bob Martinez.

Key changes from the previous policy:
- All server logs must now be retained for 90 days. Previously the limit was 30 days.
- Customer data must be retained for a minimum of 3 years per the new regulatory requirement.
- Backup snapshots are taken every 24 hours and retained for 14 days.

The engineering team, led by Sarah Park, is responsible for implementing the technical controls.
Sarah Park confirmed that the new log rotation scripts were deployed on April 1 2024.

Any exceptions to this policy must be approved by Alice Chen and documented in the policy register.
